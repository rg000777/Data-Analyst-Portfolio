# 🚗 Hyundai Consumer Behaviour Analysis

## 📋 Business Problem
Study consumer purchase behaviour toward Hyundai 
in the Indian automobile market through primary research.

## 🛠️ Framework Used
Survey Research → Consumer Behaviour Framework 
→ SWOT Analysis → Competitor Mapping

## 📊 Key Insights
- Brand trust & after-sales service = top purchase drivers
- Price sensitivity high among middle-class buyers
- Hyundai positioned between premium & budget segments
- Maruti & Tata identified as key competitors
